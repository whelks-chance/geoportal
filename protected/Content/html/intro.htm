What is the WISERD Geoportal?


The WISERD Geoportal is an online Geographic Information System (GIS) designed to enhance a researcher's ability to discover socio-economic research data relating to Wales, with the aim of encouraging collaborative research and re-use of existing data. The WISERD geoportal provides map-based and text-based search tools for accessing a rich meta-database of government surveys (e.g. Living in Wales, Welsh Health Survey) primary qualitative WISERD research (e.g. interview transcripts), administrative data (e.g. Pupil Level Annual School Census) and 'grey' data (e.g. academic publications). The database holds metadata for both quantitative and qualitative data, and is expanding constantly.



Latest News


An alpha version of the Geoportal is currently under development and is due to be demonstrated at the WISERD Annual Conference in June 2011. User-testing of the beta version to commence Summer 2011, with the a release version due at the end of 2011.
