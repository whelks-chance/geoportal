DataPortal Cookie Policy



Continued usage of the WISERD DataPortal indicates agreement with the usage of cookies within your browser.

Disabling the usage of cookies may result in deminished funtionality.



In order to provide easy, useful, and reliable services to our visitors the WISERD DataPortal places small amounts of information on your device, in order for features such as search queries to function. These include small files known as cookies. They cannot be used to identify you personally, they are only temporarily linked to your device.

Third party cookies

Additionally, external sources such as Edina (which provides the map layers within the DataPortal) places cookies to help discover how their service is being utilised.

Edinas privacy and cookie policy can be found here.
